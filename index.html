about us

Fotograma Studio is a Roblox-native animation and content studio based in British Columbia, Canada.

Fotograma means frame in Spanish — and just like how frames are all the individual pieces that put together a video, we provide you with all the pieces you need to build your presence in the Roblox metaverse.

Our focus is on 3D animation, social media, and visual storytelling. Whether it's Roblox game trailers, promotional content, or short-form content — come to us with your idea, and we will bring it to life.
