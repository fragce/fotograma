A full production house for Roblox

Fotograma Studio is an animation studio and production house specializing in cinematic 3D visuals for Roblox. With over five years of experience, we bring film-level production quality, visual storytelling, and artistic direction to every project.

how we work

our philosophy

Craft

Every project is treated like a film production, because that's what it is. Cinematography, visual storytelling, and artistic direction are our main priorities. 100% human made art. Always.

Quality

First impressions are made in seconds. We hold ourselves to the high standards of the animation industry. Every deliverable is polished, intentional, and built with meticulous attention to detail.

Collaboration

The best ideas are built together. We work closely with our clients, value open communication, and embrace new perspectives to make sure every project reaches its full creative potential.
